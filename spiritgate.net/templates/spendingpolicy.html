Spending Policy


Back to Home

  • The Spiritgate team is not a business
  • The Spiritgate team is not a charity (Then what is it?)
  • The Spiritgate team will use funds to improve Spiritgate
  • The Spiritgate team will not be financially compensated for work on Spiritgate. Time is considered donated.
  • The Spiritgate team will not have access to funds over $10 per week without a majority vote
    • If a member of the team has been absent for more than 3 days, they will not be included in the vote.
  • The Spiritgate team will not spend funds on assets that could be produced at similar quality in-house
  • The Spiritgate team will not spend funds on assets to be included in Supporter packs, except when it is unreasonable that the improved quality of the supporter pack will not net revenue
  • The Spiritgate team will not have access to funds for personal use
    • Except in extreme and immediate personal emergencies - in which case a public explanation and apology will be made. The team member drawing funds will also be expected to repay into the Spiritgate Fund as the situation allows.
  • The Spiritgate team will not treat Supporter Pack purchasers apart from other Spiritgate players
  • Donations from outside the team will not be accepted. (Reason)
  • The Spiritgate website will not store private information regarding your Credit Card, Connection (IP Address and related information), or Steam Account
  • The Spiritgate team will not surrender database information to any external party - and will attempt to wipe the database if forced to surrender it.
  • If a member of the Spiritgate team is found to be breaking any of these rules, they will no longer have access to the Spiritgate Fund for a period of time reasonable for the infraction. For more severe infractions, the member may be ejected from the team.

Close adherance to this policy will keep the moral fiber of modding, recreating a lost treasure, and non-profit work intact; while still allowing for funding to advance the project in ways that cannot be done without funding.
