Spiritgate is a DOTA 2 Total Conversion addon

This mod is a continuation of the Waystone Games's Cancelled MOBA Dawngate

Join us on /r/spiritgate


Features:

  • Custom Items and Item Shop
  • Custom UI
  • Custom Shaper Selection
  • Roles (Gladiator / Tactician / Hunter / Predator)
  • Improved Auto-Attack System
  • Map
  • Original Shapers (Many Done, some incomplete)
  • Parasite
  • Jungle
  • Spirit Wells
  • Bindings
  • Guardian
